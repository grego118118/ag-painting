What We Do

Painting Services That Hold Up

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and cabinets — cut in sharp and rolled smooth. Floors covered, outlets taped, and every room handed back clean and ready to live in.

Exterior Painting

Siding, trim, porches, and garages painted to survive New England seasons. Real prep work — scraping, patching, priming — so the finish lasts well past year one.

Deck & Fence Staining

Decks, railings, and fences stained and sealed to bring out the wood and protect it from sun, rain, and snow — so your outdoor space looks good every summer.

Small Carpentry

Rotten trim, loose boards, damaged siding — we fix the small stuff before we paint over it, so the job is actually done right instead of just covered up.
